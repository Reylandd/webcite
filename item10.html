A dip bar is a piece of fitness equipment that
consists of a U-shaped bar, usually about 25 mm
in diameter, which surrounds the user's body
at the waist. It is designed for the performance
of, and named after, the dip exercise.

Dips are typically used to train triceps but
they also work the chest. Dips also can work
your back, forearms (when holding the bars),
shoulders, core, and more, as you work to
keep balanced and in control.
