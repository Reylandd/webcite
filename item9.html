A stationary bicycle is a device used as exercise
equipment for indoor cycling. It includes a saddle,
pedals, and some form of handlebars arranged as
on a bicycle. A stationary bicycle is usually a
special-purpose exercise machine resembling a
bicycle without wheels.

Spin bikes are designed for high-intensity cycling,
and are usually used by group cycling classes.These
lean forward and have a minimal design, with the aim
being to mimic riding a real road bike. These bikes
are made for challenging workouts and usually aren't
very beginner-friendly
