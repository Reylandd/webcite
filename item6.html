A Smith machine is a weight training machine
that features a barbell between rails so
that the weight can only move on a vertical
plane. Lifting with a Smith machine boosts
stability and safety, especially during solo
workouts. Smith machines can help you work
a wide range of muscles and perform a variety
of moves.
